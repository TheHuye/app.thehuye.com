Loan applicant form

The Higher Education Council (HEC) of Rwanda offers financial aid to students attending the University of Rwanda through loans. Students are selected based on their final exam scores in high school and their chosen course of study at the University of Rwanda. There is a competitive selection process, and the number of loans awarded may vary depending on the budget allocated each year. The HEC loan covers tuition fees at the University of Rwanda. It does not typically cover living expenses or other personal costs. But a bursary of 40,000Rwf. If you want to receive the loan please fill the below form.
