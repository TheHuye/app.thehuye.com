University of Rwanda Academic Calendar For 2024-2025

the academic calendar for the 2024 academic year outlines the key dates and processes for application, registration, and academic activities. Applications for Medicine and Dental Surgery programs begin on February 5, 2024, with results announced by mid-March. General applications run from February 19 to March 19, with admission results released by April 20. New student registration starts on May 13, followed by a mandatory induction program from June 10 to August 18. Returning students can register from July 22 to August 25. The academic year kicks off on September 2.
